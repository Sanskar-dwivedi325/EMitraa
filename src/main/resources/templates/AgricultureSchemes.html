Benefits of Each Scheme

Scheme Benefits
Agricultural Subsidy Scheme Provides financial assistance to farmers for agricultural inputs and equipment.
Crop Insurance Scheme Protects farmers from crop losses due to natural disasters and other risks.
Soil Health Card Scheme Promotes soil health management through the issuance of soil health cards to farmers.
Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana Provides insurance coverage to farmers against crop losses due to various risks.
Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana Promotes organic farming and sustainable agriculture practices.
National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture Focuses on promoting sustainable agriculture practices and conserving natural resources.
Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana Aims to increase agricultural productivity, profitability, and sustainable development.
National Food Security Mission Addresses the issue of food security and malnutrition by enhancing agricultural productivity.
Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture Promotes holistic development of horticulture sector for income generation and nutritional security.
National Horticulture Mission Focuses on the development of horticulture sector for income generation and nutritional security.
Horticulture Mission for North East and Himalayan States Promotes horticulture development in the North East and Himalayan States for income generation and nutritional security.
National Scheme on Welfare of Fishermen Aims to improve the socio-economic conditions of fishermen and fish farmers.
